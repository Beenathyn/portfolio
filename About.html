About
Hi, I'm Bee! I'm an artist and designer with a passion for 2D art and characters that are just a little weird. I am currently completing my degree in Interactive Media and Game Development at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. I have experience in Adobe Photoshop and Animate, 3ds Max, and ZBrush.

My goal is to create art with meaning, and to use games as a unique medium through which to tell impactful stories.
